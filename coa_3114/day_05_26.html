May 26: Epidauros

Asklepios was a fascinating god to learn about, since he represents a change in Greek philosophy. Not only was he a god who was interested in intervening in the lives of humans by helping them, he was willing to go against the natural order of things by bringing back the dead. This was something that the Greeks previously would not have imagined their gods doing. The idea that the Greek gods were growing more powerful in the eyes of the Greeks shows a shift in the way Religion was practiced by the Greeks over time.

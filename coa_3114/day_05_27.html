May 27: Taras

Taras, or Taranto, is located in the northern tip of the "boot" of Italy. Taras would have been a military port colony of the Magna Grecia. The city was large enough to have its own agora, colossal statues of Zeus and Hercules, and at least one temple. This temple may have been a temple to Gaia or Aphrodite.

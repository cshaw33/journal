Part 1: COA 3114

Since Saint Peter is considered to be the first Pope and head of the Catholic church, Saint Peter's basilica is a very important religious building for many practicing Catholics today. The space is very majestic, if potentially overwhelming due to the vast numbers of people visiting and the extravagant decorations both outside and in. This trend continues through the rest of Vatican city today, unfortunately creating a rushed environment that prevented us from spending as much time appreciating the works there compared to other places we visited. All things considered though, the Vatican is still a marvelous place to experience.

June 19: The Duomo Complex

Brunelleschi's masterpiece is the Dome of Florence, though how the dome was constructed is a greater marvel than the dome itself. Brunelleschi was taking over work started by Ghiberti, the original architect for the project. The dome has an octagonal drum and is double layered. This helps to lighten the load of the dome itself. There were pulleys used to raise and lower piers, powered by oxen. An amazing feature of this system is that the oxen did not need to be turned around to move the piers in a different direction.

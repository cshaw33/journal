June 25: Venice

Church of Santa Maria dei Miracoli

Church of Santissimo Giovanni e Paolo

The church of Santissimo Giovanni e Paolo is the largest church in Venice after the Basilica of Saint Mark. This church is an example of Venetian Gothic, and there are many doges of Venice buried here.

Piazza San Marco

The Piazza San Marco is the only large square in Venice. Until around 1300 the space composed of two narrow squares with a canal running between them, which was filled in to create the piazza.

Basilica of San Marco

The Basilica of Saint Mark is dedicated to Mark the apostle and patron saint of the city since AD 828 when the remains of the saint were brought to Venice for protection.
